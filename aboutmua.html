    Education Just Me

I am currently enrolled at Mt. Carmel High School as a Junior (11th grade).


But my world does not only extend the the border-lines of Mount. Carmel nor San Diego.

I constantly go to visit Tijuana and Ensenada Mexico to see family, family-friends and so on.

The reason why I can go to Mexico mainly circles around the fact that both my parents are Mexican; my mother both born and raised, and my father born in the united states and raised in Mexico.

This also means I have mexican blood racing through my veins, and the ability to speak English and Spanish.

Although not a native speaker (yet), I am a proficient Spanish speaker, learning from the masters themselves.


But that is not to say I only see the mexican side of myself. I am proud to be both of Mexican blood, and have nationality in the United States of America

However, Mexico is just a piece of my life. While another piece is in America, a very special part part of me is my trip to Italy!
